What is Kathy Young's Net Worth?

Kathy Young is an American musician who has a net worth of $1 million. Kathy Young was born in Santa Ana, California in October 1945. She gained fame as a teen pop singer in the early 1960s. She was chosen to sing back-up vocals for the band The Innocents on their single "A Thousand Stars" when she was only 15 years old. The song reached #3 on the Billboard Hot 100 chart and #6 on the US R&B chart. Kathy Young released their debut studio album The Sound of Kathy Young in 1961.

She released her other Our Best to You 20 years later in 1981. Her single "Happy Birthday Blues" reached #30 on the Billboard Hot 100 chart. Young married singer-songwriter John Walker who founded The Walker Brothers but it only lasted from 1965 to 1968. She managed her family citrus ranch and made a comeback performing in the 2000s. In 2014 she was inducted into the Doo-Wop Hall of Fame.
