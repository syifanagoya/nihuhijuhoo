Allison Holker, Stephen 'tWitch' Boss' Quotes on Working Together

Publish date: 2024-06-30

Dancing duo. Allison Holker and husband Stephen ‘tWitch’ Boss sparked an instant connection after their respective So You Think You Can Dance seasons, going on to tie the knot in 2013.

The former Dancing With the Stars pro, for her part, competed on season 2 of the FOX show nearly two years before Boss auditioned for the reality TV program. While neither Holker nor the Magic Mike XXL actor won their initial seasons, they both came back as “All-Star” mentors — and enjoyed getting to work together.

“Over the years, we’ve done so much but the common denominator and the thought line has always been, like, joy and laughter and love, for sure,” Boss gushed in a joint February 2022 YouTube video with his wife, recapping their favorite duets. “So, even looking back at all these things that we were blessed enough to do, it’s, like, I think it came from as much light as we’re putting in the world.”

Holker and Boss — who share son Maddox, born in 2016, and daughter Zaia, born in 2019, as well as the Minnesota native’s daughter Weslie from a previous relationship — also took their growing romance to new heights when they began working together. Us Weekly exclusively confirmed in November 2017 that the married pair would host Freeform’s Disney’s Fairy Tale Weddings and Disney’s Fairy Tale Weddings: Holiday Magic. The docuseries, which ran between 2017 and 2020, saw Holker and Boss — who later adopted Weslie — copresent the program about Disney-held nuptials.

“Our wedding is one of my favorite moments of my life,” the HGTV personality told Us at the time. “It’s such a special moment that I got to share with my husband. When they asked us to be a part of Disney’s Fairy Tale Weddings, I was honored to be a part of someone else’s moment that they’ll remember forever. It’s so special. It just seemed like a magical moment.”

Amid growing careers, Holker and Boss continued to dance together — even during the contemporary dancer’s pregnancies with Maddox and Zaia.

“My best advice is to find a dance partner that you trust and you love. For me, that’s my beautiful wife, Allison,” he said during an October 2019 episode of The Ellen DeGeneres Show. “Listen, she is 9 months pregnant, she is my hero, she’s here and we’re about to groove like only Bosses can do!”

The former Ellen DeGeneres Show DJ and the Flirty Dancing choreographer further showcased their dance moves via social media.

“A moment with my HUS @sir_twitch_alot ! ❤️,” Holker quipped via Instagram in May 2022, sharing footage of the twosome dancing in their backyard.

Boss died the following December at the age of 40 of an apparent suicide. “It is with the heaviest of hearts that I have to share my husband Stephen has left us,” Holker said in a statement at the time. “Stephen lit up every room he stepped into. He valued family, friends and community above all else and leading with love and light was everything to him. He was the backbone of our family, the best husband and father, and an inspiration to his fans.”
