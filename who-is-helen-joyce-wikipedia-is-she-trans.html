Helen Joyce is absent from Wikipedia despite being a celebrated writer at her age. 

If you are involved in Trans activism, you may have heard about Helen Joyce. The Times quoted her new book as “a rigorous and brave book”. 

In fact, Joyce is known for shutting down homophobic and transphobic ideologies from people. 

Helen Joyce Wikipedia – Is She Trans?

Helen Joyce doesn’t have a detailed Wikipedia bio description.

Helen Joyce is a writer and author. She is working as the Executive Editor for Events at The Economist. Likewise, she is an expert in issues related to gender and sexuality.

Recently, Joyce published her long-awaited book titled Trans: When Ideology Meets Reality. She beautifully captures subjects like women’s rights, fairness in sport, and same-sex attraction in the book.

Helen Joyce Age 

Helen Joyce is a decorated writer at the age of her 50s.

She currently resides in London, England and is of British nationality. Likewise, she completed her undergraduate degree from Trinity College in Dublin. 

Unlike her journalism career, Helen is actually a mathematics major. She graduated as a gold medalist from Trinity. Furthermore, she pursued her Master’s degree in Advanced Mathematics from the University of Cambridge.

However, she did not stop here. In 1995, Joyce got her PhD in geometric measure theory, Mathematics from University College London.

Soon after her graduation, she entered into a writing career as an editor for Maths magazine at the University of Cambridge.
