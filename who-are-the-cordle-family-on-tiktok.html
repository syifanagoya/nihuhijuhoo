Who Are The Cordle Family On TikTok?

Publish date: 2024-05-29

The Cordle Family TikTok account has 3.9 million followers. It includes Devin Cordle and her husband Hunter Cordle along with their daughter Ivy.

Devin and Hunter content on TikTok and YouTube mostly focuses on their household, faith, and community. They post vlogs, day in the life, and routine-style videos. The couple also has several pranks and challenges videos on TikTok and YouTube.

It all began as an entertaining way to keep in touch while Hunter was busy at work. Their TikTok account had almost 2 million followers just within a couple of months.

Their first hit video on the app was when Hunter shaved his mustache and recorded Devin’s reaction. It was just them being themselves, and the fans loved it.

The Cordle Family Found Internet Fame In 2022

Devin and Hunter Cordle are a TikTok fam from Dothan in Alabama. Their content regarding faith, community, and everyday life at home keeps them balanced.

Devin and Hunter’s different personalities, with her priceless reactions to his deadpan delivery, always make up for fun content. Through the balance, they have been keeping it real both online and in real life.

Hunter And Devin Cordle Became TikTok Famous In Early 2022

During an interview with News 4 in March 2022, Devin revealed that she still cannot believe that all the fame is real life. Many people call them ‘Famous and TikTok famous,’ but the couple does not know if it fits them yet. They do not feel famous but instead just feel like their usual selves.

One of their most famous videos involves them arguing at a store over the pillows. Hunter insists that Devin should not be buying pillows, and she tries to convince him that they need to buy them for their new house. The video has over 25 million views and 3.9 million likes so far.

Although social media has completely changed Hunter and Devin’s lives, they take necessary breaks. Their family always comes first, and they believe there are days when, if they are not careful, the social media world can take over them.

The couple spends some time about 5-10 minutes just replying to comments and interacting with their lovely followers. They then try to shut it off and spend quality time with each other.

Devin and Hunter are glad to incorporate their small town of Dothan into their videos. The couple moved into a beautiful house in mid-2022 and gave their views a glimpse into it through a ‘House Tour’ in January 2023.

Devin Cordle And Hunter Cordle Were High School Sweethearts

Devin and Hunter got married at the age of 19. They had a beautiful wedding ceremony and celebrated with their friends and family in 2017.

They met when she was 13, and he was 14. They were each other’s dates at homecomings and proms in high school. Soon after blowing up on TikTok, the couple posted a Relationship Q&A, where they talked about how they met and their love story.

Hunter And Devin Are 6 Months Apart In Age

Devin was born on July 24, 1998, and is currently 24 years old. She was born and raised in Dothan, Alabama.

Hunter was born on February 10, 1998, and is currently 25 years old. The couple shares an age gap of about six months.

Their daughter was born when Devin was 22 and Hunter was 23.

Hunter Is A Former Firefighter

Hunter was a member of the Dothan Fire Department. He started making videos as a way to decompress after a long shift. 

He announced in October 2022 through a YouTube video that he had quit his job. He left the job to focus on social media full-time.

They posted a vlog titled, ‘Hunter’s Last Day as a Fire Fighter’ where they did answer more questions related to him quitting his job.

Devin Was A Stay At Home Mom When They Started Posting On Social Media

Devin started posting on social media to earn a little extra cash to help the household. Her husband was working overtime at the fire department to help with their growing house.

She has been balancing being a stay-at-home mom and social media. The couple has been devoting their time as content creators and traveling. 

Hunter And Devin Cordle Love Posting Family Photos On Instagram

Devin frequently posts pictures with her husband and baby on Instagram. They traveled to Hawaii in February and posted some of the loveliest pictures. The couple even posted videos on YouTube on how they packed for Hawaii and traveled with their toddler.

She has over 457k followers and 470 posts. She also has posts related to her faith, lifestyle, makeup, and various events she attends with Hunter.

Devin’s most recent post has been about Taylor and Sophia, another popular TikTok couple’s wedding. She was one of the bridesmaids at the wedding.

Hunter has over 140k followers on Instagram with 206 posts. His Instagram bio says, ‘Jesus loves you.’ He also loves posting about cars and bikes on social media.

Devin And Hunter Have A Daughter Ivy Cordle

Devin And Hunter have a two-year-old daughter named Ivy Cordle. She was born in March 2021.

Ivy had a St. Patricks Day themed birthday celebration for her first birthday. All dressed in white with a green bow headband on her head, Ivy had a blast of a celebration.

She recently traveled to Hawaii with her parents. They posted a series of photos on Instagram and even talked about how they planned and made the trip possible on YouTube.

During the pregnancy, Devin was diagnosed with gestational hypertension at 37 weeks. It led to an unexpected inducement at 37 weeks.

Devin Cordle Has Five Siblings

Cordle grew up in a large household with five siblings. In one of the YouTube videos, she talked about how she used to want 3-4 kids because she grew up in a big fam.

One of Devin’s sisters, Logan McGinnis, posted on TikTok as she announced her pregnancy to their mom and other sisters. They were all very happy to learn about the pregnancy, and everyone was in tears.
