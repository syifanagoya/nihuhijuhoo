What is Scott Rockenfield's Net Worth?

Scott Rockenfield is an American drummer and composer who has a net worth of $4 million. Scott Rockenfield was born in Seattle, Washington in June 1963. He is best known for being the drummer of the band Queensryche. Rockenfield co-founded the band and is also known for being the drummer of the band Slave to the System. Queensryche was formed in 1980 and released their debut studio album The Warning in 1984.

They released the albums Rage for Order in 1986 and Operation: Mindcrime in 1988. Their album Empire was released in 1990 and reached #7 on the Billboard 200 and was certified 3x Platinum. Queensryche released the album Promised Land in 1994 which reached #3 on the Billboard 200 chart. They have also released the albums Hear in the Now Frontier in 1997, Q2K in 1999, Tribe in 2003, Operation: Mindcrime II in 2006, Take Cover in 2007, American Soldier in 2009, Dedicated to Chaos in 2011, Queensryche in 2013, and Condition Human in 2015.
