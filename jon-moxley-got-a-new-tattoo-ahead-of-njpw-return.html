Jon Moxley Got A New Tattoo Ahead Of NJPW Return

Publish date: 2024-09-27

Jon Moxley debuted at AEW Double Or Nothing 2019 and wasted no time in making a huge impact on the company. Four years since his debut, Moxley has become a mainstay of AEW. He loves wrestling in Japan and so it comes as no surprise that Moxley ended up getting a new tattoo ahead of his NJPW return.

At the NJPW Dominion event, Jon Moxley, Claudio Castagnoli and Shota Umino challenged Kazuchika Okada & Tomohiro Ishii) & Hiroshi Tanahashi for the NEVER Openweight Six Man Tag Team Championship.

It was truly a slugfest from start to finish as fans expected no less. Jon Moxley in particular had a solid encounter with Tomohiro Ishii and fans simply lost their minds. Of course, The Blackpool Combat Club lost in the end.

As pointed out by a fan on Twitter, Jon Moxley got a tattoo on his right arm ahead of his NJPW return. Written in Katakana, it translated to ‘Puroresu,’ which is the Japanese style of pro wrestling.

We will have to wait and see what AEW has planned for Jon Moxley and the rest of the Blackpool Combat Club, as we are heading towards the AEW x NJPW Forbidden Door event on June 25th.
