Fixer to Fabulous star Joe Looneys age explored

Publish date: 2024-05-05

Fixer to Fabulous is a beloved home renovation show on HGTV, and at its heart are skilled professionals like Joe Looney. Let’s delve into Joe’s role on the show, his inspiring journey, and his age.

The Looney Legacy on Fixer to Fabulous

Joe Looney, a pivotal member of Fixer to Fabulous, plays a crucial role in the show’s construction endeavors. As Chase Looney’s father, his expertise in carpentry shines through in every project.

Family holds a special place in the Looneys’ hearts, evident in Chase’s Instagram posts. Joe’s influence on his son’s career path is undeniable, with both sharing a passion for carpentry.

From Firefighter to Master Carpenter

Joe’s journey is nothing short of remarkable. Starting as a firefighter, he instilled a sense of duty in his son, Chase, who later followed in his footsteps. As the years unfolded, Joe honed his skills to become a master carpenter, a craft that Chase has also embraced.

Joe Looney’s Age: Unraveling the Mystery

Though the exact birthdate of Joe Looney in 2021 remains unconfirmed, it’s reported by Stars Offline that he is 64 years old. Given his role as a devoted father and grandfather to Chase’s children, this estimate seems fitting.

In a heartwarming episode of Fixer to Fabulous’s holiday special on December 14th, 2021, Joe welcomed a new furry family member named Spirit. Fans adore Joe’s kind-hearted nature, making him a cherished figure on the HGTV series.

Chase’s Tribute: A Human Hero

Chase, in an Instagram post from 2020, paid a touching tribute to his father, referring to him as a “human hero.” This sentiment underscores the profound impact Joe has had on his family and those around him.

The Adoration of HGTV Fans

The adoration for Joe Looney extends far beyond his immediate circle. Fans take to platforms like Twitter to express their affection and appreciation for this beloved Fixer to Fabulous star.

One fan’s tweet encapsulates the sentiment: “I just have to know, is your dad on Twitter? I am seriously crushing on sweet Joe while watching a marathon of #FixertoFabulous.”

Joe Looney’s endearing presence on the show has undoubtedly left an indelible mark on viewers, earning him a special place in their hearts. His journey from firefighter to master carpenter is an inspiration, showcasing the power of following one’s passion.
