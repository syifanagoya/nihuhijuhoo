America's Next Top Model to return without Tyra Banks

Publish date: 2024-08-12

After their supposedly “last” cycle for the TV series, reality hit show America’s Next Top Model, is set to return on air very soon!

Recently, VH1 (via Twitter) announced the casting procedures for aspiring guys and girls to become America’s next top model.

#ANTM is NOW CASTING guys + girls! Email 2 current pics (close-up + swimsuit), contact info, age, height + weight to [email protected]!

— VH1 (@VH1) February 28, 2016

Following the announcement, word is spreading that American top model, Tyra Banks, would no longer be hosting and seating behind the judges’ table for the next cycle. But don’t fret! According to Banks, she would return as the Executive Producer and not as one of the show’s cast. Now the question, who would possibly be slipping on Tyty’s shoes as host and judge?

With the excitement for the show’s return, fans, as well as some celebs, are betting on a few renowned names in the industry (in the likes of Victoria Beckham, Gigi Hadid, Chrissy Teigen, Karlie Kloss and Coco Rocha) to fill the new panel of judges and cast for ANTM’s revival.
