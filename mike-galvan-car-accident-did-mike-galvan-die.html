Mike Galvan Car Accident: Did Mike Galvan Die?

Publish date: 2024-02-18

In December 2021, a tragic accident occurred which claimed the life of a 16-year-old lady Macie Ann Galvin.

Mike Galvan was the driver at the wheel at the time of the incident and he hails from Houston.

Mike Galvan was suspected to be driving under the influence of alcohol.

Two others also lost their lives as a result of the crash and they are; Giselle Herrera and Aliya Lopez.

It’s highly likely that Mike Galvan together with the passengers were under the influence of alcohol. However, a report has not been released to that effect.

At the time of the incident, Mike Galvan was a student senior at a high school.

Mike Galvan didn’t lose his life but his bad choice of driving under the influence had led him behind bars as his Honda was traveling at high speed on Dos Reis Road and he failed to stop when the road ended.

The Honda dived over the levee, and due to its speed, it overturned and landed on its roof in the river.

Mike Galvan survived but the rest of his crew, including two 16-year-old girls and one 17-year-old girl, were not able to survive the crash.
