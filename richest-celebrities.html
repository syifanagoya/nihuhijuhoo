What is Tim Booth's Net Worth?

Tim Booth is an English singer, dancer, and actor who has a net worth of $3 million. Tim Booth was born in Bradford, Yorkshire, England in February 1960. He is best known for being the lead singer of the band James. He released the albums Booth and the Bag Angel in 1996, Bone in 2004, and Love Life in 2011. The band James formed in 1982 and broke up in 2001 before reuniting in 2007. Their debut studio album Stutter was released in 1986.
